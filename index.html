📚 Grounded in CBT

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is one of the most evidence-backed approaches to mental health. The thought records here are adapted from CBT worksheets used by therapists worldwide. This tool is a supplement — not a replacement — for professional care.

🌱 How CBT Helps Long-Term

Studies show that regular use of cognitive reframing techniques can reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression by 40–60% over 8–12 weeks. It helps rewire unhelpful thought patterns into more balanced, compassionate ones.

💡 Quick Journaling Tips
  • Write without worrying about grammar or spelling
  • Focus on how you feel rather than what happened
  • End with one small kind sentence to yourself
